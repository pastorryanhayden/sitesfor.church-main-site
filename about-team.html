Our Team

Ryan Hayden

Ryan has served as the senior pastor at Bible Baptist Church in Mattoon, IL since 2011. Before that he was an assistant pastor, children’s pastor, Christian camp director, and Christian school teacher. He’s the host of the popular “Foolishness of Preaching Podcast” where he interviews a different preacher every week and he blogs at pastorryanhayden.com.

Ryan has been making websites since he was in Bible college in 2004. Over the last 12 years he’s made dozens of church and ministry websites for churches all around the country and the world.
