Help Churches

The vast majority of churches have less than 100 members. In most cases, these churches have neither the time, personnel or resources to have a great website. Most church websites solutions out there charge an expensive set-up fee and then $30-$50 a month.

We know from experience, $1,500 a year for a church website just isn’t doable for a small church.

Even if it was, most churches don’t have the time, knowledge or graphic design skills to KEEP a church website great after initial handoff. Want proof of this? Do a random sampling of church websites and you’ll find that in most cases, they haven’t been updated in months if not years.

We are out to fix this.

Simply put: We want to provide websites that are both easy to update and inexpensive so that any church can have a great website.

Help Ministers in Training

Our second goal may seem strange: we want to provide jobs to would-be pastors, bible college students and people just starting out in the ministry that they can do on their own time to help support their mission and their families. Each sitesfor.church website is assigned a “site-rep” - a real, honest-to-goodness human being (who lives in the same hemisphere as you) who will help your church set-up and customize your website and who will serve as your primary support contact.

Think back to when you were a student (or just starting out) - would you have liked to have a side job that fit with your mission in life (helping churches) that you could do on your own time and that paid well? Of course you would.

Using site-reps like this not only gives you a better, more personal experience it also helps real people who are trying to serve God with their life.

Revolutionize church websites

We have big goals. We want to become the go-to company for church websites. We know what we have right now isn’t for everyone. But we are pushing toward fixing that. Here are some of the things that are in the pipeline:

A. A custom CMS designed for churches

Airtable is great - but it’s not our end game. We want to make a custom CMS designed from scratch for churches. We’ve already got a prototype in the works and it is going to be awesome!

B. Custom iOS and Android apps for churches

We want you to have your own app. Imagine telling your members they can skip the paper bulletin and just download the ^Name of your Church^ App on the App store or Google Play. Not only will this give your church members access to prayer requests, sermons, events, etc. on their phones - it will also allow you to easily send notifications to most of the people in your church.

C. Bulletins and Prayer Sheets

As part of our eventual CMS - we are going to provide an easy way to make printable prayer sheets and bulletins. These will be simple and data-driven but will potentially save you tons of time. (Ryan’s church already does some bulletins this way.)

D. Websites for missionaries

Eventually, we want to offer websites for missionaries as well. This is something we already have a lot of experience in. Missionary websites would be even cheaper than church websites (we aren’t interested in ANY profit from missionaries). For missionary websites - we plan to find and use national pastors (who have tech skills) as site-reps. We are already looking for these men and working on missionary themes.
