Working With About Pages#


Note

Updates: We've added several new page types to about pages, with special bases for a Leadership page (i.e. our pastor), a Beliefs page, and a Gospel presentation page. You can still add as many other pages as you want using the "Other_Pages" tab.

The Leadership Page#

The staff page is designed to allow you to easily showcase your pastor, staff and even volunteers on your website.

Each row in the leadership base showcases one leader. Rows will be displayed in the order they show on Airtable with one exception. The Senior Pastor (Lead Pastor or whoever your main preacher is) will allways be displayed at the top of the page and will have some extra styling for emphasis.

The Beliefs Page#

In order to make your statement of faith a little more digestible, we've added some special styles and icons for each section. The icons come from font awesome and you can use any of the icons they provide (simply type the icon name, starting with "fa" - i.e. "fa-icon-name" into the field). To make it simpler, we've provided you with a premade list of icons that we find work best with a statement of faith.

The Content field accepts markdown.

The Gospel Page#

The gospel page is a single-row base for displaying a gospel presentation on your site. You can change the heading and featured image (a 16x9 image) and the content of the text using markdown.

We plan on eventually adding several gospel presentations for you to choose from. The one in the TOP row will be the one that displays on your site.

Adding Other Pages#

You can add as many other "About Us" pages as you want. Simply add them to the "Other Pages" tab.

About pages couldn't be simpler. There are just just four fields:

  • Title
  • Body_content
  • Featured_image
  • published.

Just remember that, like every segment of the site, "Body_content" needs to be written in markdown and the "featured_image" needs to be a 16x9 (widescreen) image.
