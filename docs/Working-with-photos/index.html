Working With Photos#

Adding Galleries#

Depending on your theme, gallery images will be shown in these places:

  • On the gallery page.
  • On ministry pages.
  • On event pages. (coming soon)
  • On the home page (on some themes)

Photos are added through the "Photos" tab, of the "Ministry base."

To add photos, simply work through either the grid view or form view being sure to:

1. Give the gallery a title.#

The title will display above each individual gallery on the gallery page but will not show up anywhere else.

2. Upload your photos.#

You can upload photos by dragging and dropping files onto the "gallery" cell. Please note: You can and should add multiple photos to one cell. Each row is for one gallery not for one photo.

3. Choose events and ministries this gallery belongs to.#

This is optional. If you want the galleries to show up on ministry or event pages, choose the appropriate ministries or events from the ministry and events fields.

Note

Pro Tip: Picassa, Instagram, Flickr, or Dropbox for photos.

One neat trick with photos is made possibly by Airtable's Picassa (Google Photos), Instagram, Flickr and Dropbox integrations. If you use any of these services to manage your photos, you can easily access them to add to your galleries. If you are primarily a phone photographer (and most people are) then this will save you a lot of time.
